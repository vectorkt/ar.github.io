Are you tired of wading through virtual chests of worthless items only to get to the good stuff? Wouldn't it be nice if we could automate the boring stuff in video games? Autolooter is a C++11 application which separates the wheat from the chaff. In english, it looks at items from an input(currently a text file) and takes the most profitable based on a greedy approach. To avoid protability issues, you can run it directly here.
